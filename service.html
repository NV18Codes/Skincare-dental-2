    Hair Transplant

    Description: FUE technology for natural and permanent hair restoration.



    Hair Transplant Benefits:

    Permanent solution to hair loss

    Natural-looking results

    No need for continuous treatment after healing


    Hair Transplant Treatments Types:

    FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction): Individual hair follicles are extracted and implanted; minimal scarring.

    FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation): A strip of scalp is removed, and follicles are transplanted; suitable for larger areas.

    PRP Therapy

    Description: Stimulates hair regrowth using plasma-derived growth factors.



    PRP Therapy Benefits:

    Stimulates Hair Growth

    Strengthens Existing Hair

    Minimally Invasive & Safe


    How PRP Therapy Works

    Blood Draw : A small amount of blood (typically from your arm) is drawn, similar to a routine blood test.

    Centrifugation (Separation Process) : The blood is placed in a centrifuge, which spins at high speeds to separate the platelet-rich plasma from other components.

    Hair Diagnosis

    Description:Customized treatment plans based on a detailed scalp analysis.



    Benefits of a Hair Diagnosis:

    Helps identify the root cause of hair problems

    Allows for personalized treatment plans

    Prevents unnecessary treatments or products


    What Happens After a Hair Diagnosis?

    Medications: Minoxidil, Finasteride, or anti-dandruff treatments

    Nutritional Changes: Supplements like biotin, iron, or vitamin D

    Scalp Treatments: PRP therapy, mesotherapy, or laser therapy

    Acne Scar & Pigmentation

    Description: Effective laser and micro-needling treatments for a flawless complexion.



    Best Treatments for Acne Scars & Pigmentation

    Exfoliation: Helps remove dead skin cells and fade marks.

    Vitamin C Serum: Brightens skin and reduces dark spots.

    Retinol (Vitamin A): Stimulates collagen and improves skin texture.


    How to Prevent Acne Scars & Pigmentation

    Treat acne early to prevent deep scars.

    Avoid picking or popping pimples (prevents scarring).

    Use sunscreen daily to stop dark spots from worsening.

    Skin Brightening

    Description: Safe and specialized procedures to enhance your skin tone.



    Why Does Skin Lose Its Glow?

    Sun Damage: UV rays cause dark spots and pigmentation.

    Dehydration: Lack of moisture makes skin look lifeless.

    Dead Skin Buildup: Leads to clogged pores and rough texture.


    Best Skincare Ingredients for Skin Brightening

    Vitamin C 11: Antioxidant that brightens skin and reduces pigmentation.

    Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) : Fades dark spots and boosts skin barrier.

    Alpha Arbutin : Lightens hyperpigmentation without irritation.

    How Do Braces Work?

    Brackets are attached to each tooth.

    Archwire connects the brackets and applies pressure.

    Elastics (Rubber Bands) help fix bite problems.


    Types of Braces & Orthodontic Treatments

    Traditional Metal Braces

    Ceramic Braces (Clear Braces)

    Lingual Braces (Hidden Braces)

    Invisalign (Clear Aligners)

    Self-Ligating Braces

    Zirconia Crowns

    Description:Strong, aesthetic crowns for long-lasting dental solutions.



    Types of Zirconia Crowns

    Solid Zirconia (Monolithic Zirconia)

    High Translucent Zirconia (Aesthetic Zirconia)

    Teeth Whitening

    Description: Professional treatments for a brighter and healthier smile.



    Types of Teeth Whitening

    Performed by a dentist using high-concentration peroxide-based gels.

    Often combined with laser or LED light for faster results.

    Can whiten teeth by 5-8 shades in one session.


    Over-the-Counter Whitening Products

    Whitening Toothpaste : Removes surface stains but does not change natural tooth color.

    Whitening Strips : Thin strips coated with peroxide gel, used daily for 1–2 weeks.

    Whitening Pens & Gels : Quick application but mild effects.
